About Me

I graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a degree in Aerospace Engineering. During college, I worked as a kitchen manager and maintenance technician at my housing co-op (did a lot of work plumbing or replacing toilets). Upon graduation, I worked developing testing equipment and solutions for commercial drones. Today I work as a corporate engineer in an oil and gas service company, where I specialize in our company's domestic and international special projects. These projects have spanned from equipment design to software development.

I started programming in college using MATLAB where I programmed a GPS receiver on my laptop. Recently, I rediscovered my love for programming by designing and programming an alert control system for an inspection pipe yard with a Beckhoff PLC and TwinCAT 3 solution.

I enjoy solving problems, designing and creating things, and have a knack of fixing things. I love the immediate satisfaction programming gives as opposed to waiting 6 months to see a mechanical design come to life.
